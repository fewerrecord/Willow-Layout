SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy describes how Willowcreek Cafe collects and uses mobile phone numbers and related information as part of its internal staff SMS notification program ("the Program").

Who this applies to

The Program is used exclusively by current Willowcreek Cafe employees (servers) for internal, work-related table-status notifications. It is not a customer-facing or public messaging program.

Information we collect

We collect the mobile phone number an employee provides during onboarding, along with their consent status (opted in, opted out) and message delivery records.

How we use this information

Mobile phone numbers are used solely to send work-related SMS alerts notifying servers when a table at their assigned station has been seated, along with related opt-in confirmation, help, and opt-out messages.

Information sharing

No mobile information collected through this Program will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes, under any circumstance. Mobile numbers and consent data may be shared with our SMS service provider (Twilio) solely to deliver the Program's messages, and are not sold or shared with any other third party.

Opting out

Employees may opt out of the Program at any time by replying STOP to any message. Once opted out, no further messages will be sent unless the employee re-enrolls.

Data retention

Phone numbers and consent records are retained for as long as an employee is active in the Program, and are removed upon request or upon the employee's departure from Willowcreek Cafe.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to Willowcreek Cafe management.
