SMS Terms & Conditions

Last updated: June 2026

These terms govern participation in Willowcreek Cafe's internal staff SMS notification program ("the Program").

Program description

The Program sends automated text messages to enrolled Willowcreek Cafe servers when a table at their assigned station has been seated, along with related opt-in confirmation, help, and opt-out messages. The Program is for internal staff use only and is not available to the public.

Enrollment

A manager enters an employee's mobile number into the Willowcreek Cafe staff app during onboarding. The employee then receives a text message and must reply YES to confirm consent before any further messages are sent.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on shift activity and table volume. There is no fixed number of messages per month.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, depending on the employee's mobile carrier and plan.

Opting out

Employees may reply STOP at any time to cancel their enrollment in the Program. Replying HELP will provide contact information for assistance.

Changes

Willowcreek Cafe may update these terms from time to time. Continued participation in the Program after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Contact

Questions about these terms can be directed to Willowcreek Cafe management.
