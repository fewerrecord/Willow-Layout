Program name: Willowcreek Cafe Table Alerts. Description: Willowcreek Cafe uses this program to send automated text messages to its own servers, notifying them when a table has been seated at their assigned station during their shift. This is an internal staff-notification tool, not a consumer marketing program, and recipients are limited to current Willowcreek Cafe employees. SMS alerts are an optional convenience only — signing up is never required to do your job, and opting in or out has no effect on your access to any other part of the app.

How to opt in: a server who wants text alerts visits the SMS sign-up page, enters their name and mobile number, and checks a box agreeing to these terms. The server then receives a one-time text and must reply YES to confirm consent before any alert texts are sent.

Message frequency varies with shift activity, typically several messages per shift. Message and data rates may apply. For help, reply HELP. To opt out at any time, reply STOP — you will receive no further messages. See our Privacy Policy.
